Serving Delicious Food for Your Family

At our restaurant, we believe that food is more than just a meal — it is a way of connecting people, creating memories, and celebrating togetherness. Every dish we prepare carries the essence of tradition, the freshness of carefully chosen ingredients, and the passion of our chefs who pour their heart into every recipe. We are committed to serving food that is not only delicious but also wholesome and satisfying for the entire family.
From aromatic biriyanis and juicy kebabs to comforting snacks and refreshing drinks, our menu is designed to offer something for everyone. Whether you’re enjoying a simple weekday dinner, treating your loved ones on the weekend, or celebrating a special occasion, we make sure that every plate served brings joy to the table.
We blend authentic flavors with a modern touch, making each bite feel like home. Our space brings families and friends together to enjoy food made with love. For us, meals are more than food — they create joy, bonds, and memories.

Mutton Dum Biriyani (Hydarabadi)

A royal delicacy, slow-cooked with tender mutton, fragrant basmati rice, golden caramelized onions, and a rich blend of authentic spices. Straight from the kitchens of the Nizams, this biryani is a timeless feast crafted to perfection for true biryani lovers.

Chicken Dum Biryani (Hyderabadi)

A royal delicacy, slow-cooked with tender chicken, fragrant basmati rice, golden caramelized onions, and a rich blend of authentic Hyderabadi spices. A timeless feast from the City of Nizams, crafted to perfection for true biryani lovers.

Mutton Dum Biryani (ChefD Special – Jeera Samba Rice)

A signature ChefD delicacy, crafted with succulent mutton and the aromatic richness of Jeera Samba rice. Slow-cooked with caramelized onions, traditional spices, and perfected over dum, this biryani offers an authentic taste of heritage in every bite.

Chicken Kebab (Chicken 65)

A fiery South Indian favorite made with tender chicken, marinated in aromatic spices, and deep-fried to golden perfection. Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside — a must-try starter for spice lovers.

Chicken Chilli (Chinese Style)

Tender chicken tossed with onions, capsicum, and a flavorful blend of Chinese sauces. A perfect Indo-Chinese starter that’s spicy, tangy, and irresistibly delicious.

Veg Manchurian

Crispy vegetable balls tossed in a rich, tangy Indo-Chinese sauce with garlic, spring onions, and spices. A perfect blend of crunch and flavor that’s loved by all.
