About Us

Variety of Biriyanis

At ChefD, we offer a rich selection of authentic biriyanis. Our menu includes Mutton Dum Biriyani, Chicken Dum Biriyani, Egg Biriyani, and Veg Biriyani. Each is slow-cooked with fresh ingredients and traditional spices to bring you unmatched flavor and aroma.

Delicious Kebabs

Our juicy and tender kebabs are marinated with traditional spices and grilled to perfection. Whether enjoyed as a starter or a main, they bring out the true flavors of ChefD’s kitchen.

Family Dining

ChefD is more than just food — it’s about sharing moments. Our warm and welcoming space is perfect for families and friends to gather, enjoy hearty meals, and create lasting memories together.

Our Mission

At ChefD, our mission is to serve authentic, flavorful food that brings families and friends together. We are committed to maintaining the highest quality standards, using fresh ingredients, and creating memorable dining experiences with every meal.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make ChefD a household name for biriyanis and traditional delicacies. We aim to spread the rich taste of our cuisine across communities while becoming a trusted destination for food made with passion and love.

ChefD serves authentic flavors with passion and care, bringing you delicious meals made with fresh ingredients.
