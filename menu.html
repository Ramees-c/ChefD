Menu

Signature Biryanis

Mutton Dum Biryani (ChefD Special)

A signature ChefD delicacy, crafted with succulent mutton and the aromatic richness of Jeera Samba rice. Slow-cooked with caramelized onions, traditional spices, and perfected over dum, this biryani offers an authentic taste of heritage in every bite.

Chicken Dum Biryani (ChefD Special)

A ChefD signature, featuring tender chicken and the unique aroma of Jeera Samba rice. Slow-cooked on dum with caramelized onions and authentic spices, this biryani delivers a rich and flavorful experience that captures true Hyderabadi tradition.

Non-Veg Starters

Chicken Kebab (Chicken 65)

A fiery South Indian favorite made with tender chicken, marinated in aromatic spices, and deep-fried to golden perfection. Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside — a must-try starter for spice lovers.

Chicken Chilli (Chinese Style)

Tender chicken tossed with onions, capsicum, and a flavorful blend of Chinese sauces. A perfect Indo-Chinese starter that’s spicy, tangy, and irresistibly delicious.

Veg Starters

Veg Manchurian

Crispy vegetable balls tossed in a rich, tangy Indo-Chinese sauce with garlic, spring onions, and spices. A perfect blend of crunch and flavor that’s loved by all.

Mushroom Manchurian

Crispy fried mushrooms tossed in a spicy Indo-Chinese sauce with onions, garlic, and spring onions. A flavorful treat for mushroom lovers.

Paneer Manchurian

Soft paneer cubes cooked in a rich Manchurian sauce with garlic and bell peppers. A delicious Indo-Chinese favorite.

Shanghai Potato

Crispy potato fingers tossed in spicy Shanghai-style sauce with garlic and chili. A perfect crunchy starter.

Indo-Chinese Specials

Chicken Noodles

Stir-fried noodles with juicy chicken, vegetables, and soy sauce for an authentic Indo-Chinese flavor.

Prawn Noodles

Flavorful noodles stir-fried with fresh prawns, vegetables, and savory sauces for a delightful seafood twist.

Veg Noodles

A classic mix of noodles tossed with seasonal veggies, soy sauce, and Asian spices for a wholesome meal.

Chicken Fried Rice

Wok-tossed basmati rice with juicy chicken, scrambled eggs, and soy sauce. A must-try Indo-Chinese classic.

Prawn Fried Rice

Fragrant fried rice with succulent prawns, spring onions, and a touch of garlic soy sauce. Perfect for seafood lovers.

Veg Fried Rice

Basmati rice tossed with fresh vegetables, garlic, and soy sauce. A healthy and tasty option.

Quick Snacks

French Fries

Crispy golden potato fries, perfectly seasoned. A classic snack loved by all ages.

Cheese Fries

Classic crispy fries topped with melted cheese for an indulgent snack.

South Indian Snacks

Chilli Bajji

Spicy green chilies dipped in seasoned batter and deep-fried until crispy. A favorite South Indian street snack.

Aloo Bonda

Deep-fried potato dumplings coated in gram flour batter. Crispy outside, soft inside, and full of flavor.

Vada

A traditional South Indian savory doughnut made with lentils, herbs, and spices. Crispy and fluffy.

Samosa

Crispy golden pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas. A beloved Indian snack.

Pakkuvada

Crispy South Indian fritters made from gram flour and onions, deep-fried to perfection. A crunchy tea-time snack.

Parippu Vada

A traditional Kerala snack made with spiced lentils, fried until golden and crispy. Perfect with hot tea.

Tea

Refreshing hot tea brewed to perfection. A comforting choice anytime.

Fresh Juices
